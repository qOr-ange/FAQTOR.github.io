2. "How do I stay motivated to keep learning?"
{

   Set small, achievable goals. Programming can feel overwhelming at first,
   so celebrate small wins—like solving a coding challenge
   or creating your first app. 
   
   share your progress.
   people; even if they dont reply, still love to see your progress.
   with time this builds up and they start to reply and give more input
   as they see the project evolve.
   this is what drives the communities to truly apprecciate what you
   do and the journey there.

   Building projects that solve real problems
   or help with your daily life can make learning more rewarding.
}
