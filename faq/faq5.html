5. "I want to learn xyz"
{
   When wanting to learn something new, you can start with:
1. searching for fundamentals of the topic using any Search Engine, 
   YouTube, or online learning platforms, Wiki's and Documentation'.
2. Check if roadmap.sh has a relevant roadmap for the topic you're interested in; 
   these roadmaps can help structure your learning journey.
3. Explore dedicated resources and communities for further learning. ask.
   if you do not know what to ask, ask what you should be asking.

   Remember to learn at your own pace. 
   It's essential to give yourself the time and space to absorb new information. 
   Taking breaks is not just beneficial; it is essential for maintaining healthy balance with studying. 
   Go take a walk, lay in the grass, take a hammock with you to the park on a nice saturday.
   or catch up with latest whatever media.
   
   also using what you are learning in practical use,
   outside of learning enviroment can help further with retaining the topics long term.
   one can only get so far with comfy practice and test/example enviroments.
