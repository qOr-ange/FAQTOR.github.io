0. "What programming language should I learn?"
{
The language you choose depends on what you want to do.
   Everyone will recommend a language they’re comfortable with,
   but in the end, it depends on what excites you.
   Don’t overthink the choice—focus on learning the concepts,
   and the language will follow.

   Most programming languages share the same or very similar core concepts.
   Knowing these means you can adapt to any language later on if your first
   choice isn’t what you need.
   
   there are so many awesome-programming-languages to learn!
}
